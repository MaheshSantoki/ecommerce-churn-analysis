# E-Commerce Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview
End-to-end Business Analytics project analysing customer churn for an e-commerce platform.
Built to demonstrate RFM segmentation, machine learning, and business intelligence skills.

## Key Results
- **AUC-ROC Score: 0.99** — Random Forest churn prediction model
- **€152,031 total revenue at risk** identified across customer segments
- **Mobile Phone category** has highest churn rate at 27.5%
- **Top churn driver: Tenure** — newer customers churn significantly more

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python** — Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Seaborn, Matplotlib
- **Machine Learning** — Random Forest Classifier
- **Business Intelligence** — Power BI (3-page interactive dashboard)
- **Data** — E-Commerce Customer Churn dataset (5,630 customers, 20 features)

## Project Structure
- RFM segmentation (Champions, Loyal, At Risk, Lost)
- Churn prediction model (80%+ AUC-ROC target — achieved 0.99)
- Revenue-at-risk quantification per segment
- Power BI dashboard with executive summary, segment analysis, churn deep-dive

## Dashboard Pages
- **Page 1** — Executive Summary (KPIs, revenue at risk)
- **Page 2** — Customer Segments (RFM breakdown, churn by category)
- **Page 3** — Churn Risk Deep-dive (scatter plot, top 20 high risk customers)

## Business Recommendations
1. Target Mobile Phone customers with retention offers — highest churn & revenue risk
2. Focus on customers with Tenure < 6 months — most likely to churn
3. Increase cashback incentives for At Risk segment to recover €152K revenue
